### **2. Short Description / Purpose**

* This project focuses on analyzing smartphone sales, performance, and customer trends using Power BI.
* The goal is to convert raw data into meaningful insights for better business decision-making.
* It helps in understanding sales performance, brand comparison, pricing trends, and customer preferences.
* Designed to support stakeholders in improving product strategy and market positioning.

---

### **3. Tech Stack**

* **Power BI Desktop** – Data visualization and dashboard creation
* **Power Query** – Data cleaning and transformation
* **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Calculations and measures
* **Microsoft Excel / CSV** – Data storage and preprocessing
* **Data Modeling** – Relationship building between tables

---

### **4. Data Source**

* Smartphone sales dataset (Excel / CSV files)
* Data includes:

  * Brand and Model details
  * Price and Discounts
  * Sales Quantity and Revenue
  * Customer Ratings & Reviews
  * Region / Location-based sales
* Data may be collected from:

  * Online datasets (Kaggle, etc.)
  * Company sales records (if applicable)

---

### **5. Features and Highlights**

* 📊 Interactive dashboard with filters and slicers
* 📈 Sales trend analysis (monthly/yearly)
* 📱 Brand-wise and model-wise performance comparison
* 💰 Revenue and profit analysis
* ⭐ Customer rating and feedback insights
* 🌍 Region-wise sales distribution (maps or charts)
* 🔍 Drill-down and drill-through features for deep analysis
* 📌 KPI cards showing total sales, revenue, and top-performing products
